- feat: `centinela audit` baseline+ratchet — `centinela audit baseline` records the repo's current gate violations as a committed, deterministic baseline (`.workflow/audit-baseline.json`), and `centinela audit` then fails only on NEW violations while tolerating baselined ones and pruning resolved ones (ratchet only tightens). Enables adopting Centinela's gates on legacy codebases without a big-bang cleanup. Configurable via `[gates.audit_baseline]` (default off/warn); also runnable as a gate inside `centinela validate`. `--json` verdict for tooling.
